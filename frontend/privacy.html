Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Your privacy is important to us. It is Aura's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our website and applications.

1. Information We Collect

We only ask for personal information when we truly need it to provide a service to you. We collect it by fair and lawful means, with your knowledge and consent. The data we collect includes:

  • Account Information: Email addresses and display names provided during registration via Firebase Authentication.
  • Submitted Text: Text submitted to the Sentiment Engine for analysis is stored securely in our Firestore database to provide you with your personal history and dashboard trends.

2. How We Use Your Data

The information we collect is used strictly for providing and improving the Aura Sentiment Engine. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Generate accurate NLP sentiment, emotion, and entity-extraction reports.
  • Maintain your personalized history dashboard.
  • Provide account recovery and authentication services.

3. Data Security & Third Parties

We do not share any personally identifying information publicly or with third-parties, except when required to by law. Our backend relies on enterprise-grade infrastructure:

  • Google Cloud & Firebase: All authentication and database records are securely hosted utilizing Google's Firebase architecture.
  • No External NLP APIs: All text processing is conducted locally on our Python FastAPI server using open-source libraries (spaCy, TextBlob). Your text is never sent to third-party AI companies like OpenAI or Anthropic.

4. Your Rights

You are free to refuse our request for your personal information, with the understanding that we may be unable to provide you with some of your desired services. You may request the deletion of your account and associated analysis history at any time.

Your continued use of our website will be regarded as acceptance of our practices around privacy and personal information. If you have any questions about how we handle user data and personal information, feel free to contact us.
